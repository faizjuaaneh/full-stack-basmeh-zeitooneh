Women’s Workshop

The women’s workshops aim to empower women who currently provide financial support to their families and to help restore their sense of dignity. The workshops provide training in embroidery and subsequently allow the women an opportunity to sell their products. The programs are active in Shatilla and Burj al Barajneh. In addition to skills building, the workshops offer women awareness sessions on topics ranging from general health to women’s empowerment and women’s rights and responsibilities in the workplace.
